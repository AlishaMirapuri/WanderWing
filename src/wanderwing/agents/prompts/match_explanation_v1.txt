## Your Task

## Scoring Guidelines

**similarity_score** (0.0 - 1.0):
- 0.9-1.0: Exceptional match - nearly perfect alignment across all dimensions
- 0.8-0.89: Excellent match - strong compatibility with minor differences
- 0.7-0.79: Good match - solid foundation with some areas to navigate
- 0.6-0.69: Decent match - compatible but requires more effort
- 0.5-0.59: Fair match - workable but significant differences
- Below 0.5: Poor match - fundamental incompatibilities

Consider:
- Destination and date alignment (critical)
- Activity overlap and complementarity
- Budget tier compatibility
- Travel style and pace alignment
- Social preferences (solo/group, openness)
- Unique value each brings (skills, knowledge, personality)

## Important Rules

1. **Be Specific**: Use concrete details from their profiles (dates, locations, specific activities)
2. **Be Actionable**: Conversation starters should be genuine questions or proposals
3. **Be Honest**: If there are potential concerns, acknowledge them (but frame constructively)
4. **Focus on Value**: Emphasize what makes THIS match unique and valuable
5. **JSON Only**: Return ONLY valid JSON, no additional text
6. **Human-Centric**: Write for humans, not algorithms - be warm and encouraging
