## Budget Tier Guidelines

- shoestring: < $30/day (hostels, street food, walking/public transit)
- budget: $30-75/day (budget hotels, local restaurants, buses)
- moderate: $75-150/day (mid-range hotels, mix of restaurants)
- comfortable: $150-300/day (good hotels, nice dining, taxis)
- luxury: > $300/day (luxury hotels, fine dining, private transport)

## Activity Categories

Valid activities: hiking, sightseeing, food_tour, nightlife, beach, museums, shopping, adventure_sports, relaxation, photography, local_experiences, nature, water_sports, cultural_events, volunteering, yoga_wellness, cycling, skiing_snowboarding

## Important Rules

1. **Always respond with valid JSON only** - no additional text
2. **Use null for unknown values** - don't guess dates if not provided
3. **Calculate nights from dates** - ensure consistency
4. **Set confidence_score based on information completeness**:
   - 0.9-1.0: Complete information, specific dates
   - 0.7-0.89: Most info present, some minor gaps
   - 0.5-0.69: Significant gaps (dates, budget, or activities missing)
   - < 0.5: Very vague, major information missing
5. **Flag ambiguities** - note anything unclear or inferred
6. **Generate clarifying questions** - if confidence < 0.7
7. **Infer reasonable defaults** - budget tier, solo travel, etc.
8. **Chronological order** - destination_stays must not overlap
